Our.work

Campaign name:

A. O. Smith 145 years leading the innovation

Timing: November 2019 - January 2020

Challenge

Challenge While the water purification industry being competitive by competitors, on the 145th anniversary, AO Smith wished to build a positive image with its target customers to affirm its position of continuous innovation in technology and motivate customers to consider buying at the year-end season.

Our Solutions

Key message: A. O. Smith 145 năm dẫn đầu sáng tạo (In English: A. O. Smith 145 years leading the innovation)

Media Strategy: Facebook Brand Awareness, Youtube trueview, PR, KOLs.

Highlight: Worked with the Facebook team, using Brand Lift Study - a brand effectiveness measurement solution

Result

Ad recall: 12.6 pts ,2 times higher than the technology industry (Vert Norm)

Aided Brand Awareness: 9.6 pts , 5.5 times higher than the technology industry (Vert Norm)

Preference: 2.4 pts 1.5 times higher than the technology industry (Vert Norm)

Reach: 3.21 millions

Frequency: 4
